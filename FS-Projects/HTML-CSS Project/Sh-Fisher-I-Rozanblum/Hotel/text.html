The combination between the vast desert nature and the extraordinary architecture, Turned the prestigious Berashit Hotel into one of the unique hotels in Israel. A vacation at the Bereshit Hotel in Mitzpe Ramon guarantees an unforgettable hospitality experience - Service at the highest standards, gourmet restaurant, luxury spa, spacious rooms, A heated pool that won the title of 'the most beautiful pool in the world', And as mentioned - a desert view that has no equal, emanating from every corner. Bereshit Hotel, which covers about 50 dunams, is built of 111 accommodation villas scattered over the surface. The 39 units have private pools attached to the ground - and the upper rooms have balconies overlooking the view. The special location of Berashit Hotel brings together meticulous luxury with breathtaking nature, and guarantees pleasant and comfortable weather all year round.
• From the Isrotel Exclusive hotel group
• A luxurious, quiet and secluded vacation in the heart of the desert, on the edge of the Ramon Crater
• Winner of the title "the most beautiful pool in the world" according to the Travel Agent Central global magazine
• 111 villas and accommodation units overlooking the desert landscape, some with private pools
• The prestigious "Carmel Forests" spa, which offers 35 body, health and beauty treatments
• "Rosemaryn" restaurant, offers gourmet meals from local ingredients, from the farms around the hotel
The pool complex is open to guests throughout the year. The outdoor pool is closed for swimming in the months of January - February

Common questions

Where is the hotel located?

Bershit Hotel, Derech Bershit 1, Mitzpe Ramon, zip code: 8060

Reception times at the hotel

Sunday - Friday: arrival at the hotel from 13:00 (early entry at the hotel gate will not be possible), room check-in from 15:00. Rooms vacated by 12:00. Saturdays and holidays: arrival at the hotel starting at 4:00 p.m. (early entry at the hotel gate will not be possible), room check-in starting at 6:00 p.m. Rooms vacated by: 14:00. For more information please contact the hotel or customer service.

Is there parking?

The hotel has free parking and disabled parking. Parking is in the peripheral parking lots, from where the guests are driven to the main building in the golf courses.

Are there electric charging stations?

In the parking lot there are 4 EVEDGE electric vehicle charging points
